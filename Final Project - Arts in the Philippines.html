Final Project:

TOPIC: Arts in the Philippines

LEVEL: Primary 3

The goal of this project is to impart knowledge to third grade students about the Arts in the Philippines. This topic is in line with the curriculum of the grade three students in their Arts subject. The information about the Philippine culture is introduced through Arts. In this way, they will be aware of how different forms of art are practiced in other cultures, in this case, the Philippines. It would be helpful as they will be learning through an in depth discussion with the help of a native of the said country. This topic is beneficial, not only to third graders but to other levels as well.

Lesson Objectives:
  • Students will be aware of the arts and culture in the Philippines
  • Students will learn about how art is practiced in the Philippines
  • Students will learn about the history and different forms of art in the Philippines
  • Students will be introduced to different art products from the Philippines
  • Students will be able to implement the different practices that they have learned in the lesson
Materials Used:
  • Slide Presentation (including visuals of the different art forms in the Philippines)
  • Demonstration supplies
  • Notebooks and other classroom learning tools
Lesson Timing:
  • The content of this lesson will take place over one (1) week
  • Role of the teacher: The teacher will give an introduction about art and its essence in daily life situations
  • Role of the Language Assistant: The Language Assistant will further discuss about the topic and initiate questions to test the students comprehension regarding the discussion
